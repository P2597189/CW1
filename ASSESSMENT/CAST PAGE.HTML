    Datuk Fang Shilong SBS MBE PMW, born Chan Kong-sang and known professionally as Jackie Chan, is a Hong Kong actor, director, martial artist and stuntman known for his slapstick acrobatic fighting style, comic timing, and innovative stunts, which he typically performs himself.
    Jackie started his acting career in the year 1962 playing a 10 year old child and still is acting today. He has featured in many amazing films such as the Rush Hour trilogy, The spy next door, Karate Kid, Drunken Master and many more. In an interview featuring Chris tucker, Jackie talks about the filming he did during Rush hour 3 and how the stunts he does isnt super hero type things however, it is a bit beyond normal human being things as stated.









    Christopher Tucker is an American comedian and actor. Tucker made his debut as a stand-up performer on the HBO comedy series Def Comedy Jam, frequently appearing on the show throughout the 1990s

    Chris Tucker started his acting career in the year 1991 in his debut film Boyz n the Hood, Friday featuring another iconic Actor/Rapper "ice cube". Chris tucker was rumoured to be making and producing another Rush hour film making it the fourth film in the Rush hour Franchise. In the same interview with Jackie Chan, Tucker talks about how he might have a language barrier whilst making the film and that they cannot be funny whilst acting.
